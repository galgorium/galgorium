Working Process

My work process is characterized by a combination of strategic planning, meticulous design, precise development and extensive testing.

01.

Research and Analysis

Before starting any project, I spend time understanding the client's needs, researching the target market, and analyzing the competition. This phase is crucial to establish clear objectives and define the direction of the project.

02.

Design and Prototyping

With a solid understanding of the client's requirements, I move on to the design phase. Here, I use modern design tools to create wireframes and prototypes that visualize the structure and interaction of the website. I collaborate closely with the client to ensure the design reflects their vision and meets their expectations.

03.

Frontend and Backend development

Once the design is approved, I begin the development of the website. I use the latest technologies and best practices to build the user interface (frontend) and server logic (backend). My focus is always on efficiency, scalability and security.

04.

Testing and Optimization

Before release, I conduct extensive testing on different devices and browsers to ensure compatibility and smooth functionality. Additionally, I optimize website performance for fast loading and optimal user experience.

05.

Launch and Maintenance

Once the website is ready, I coordinate the launch and provide ongoing support for any post-launch issues. Additionally, I offer maintenance services to ensure the website remains secure and up-to-date at all times.
